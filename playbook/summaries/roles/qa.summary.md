# QA Role Summary

Use this role for independent pre-delivery app validation after product
acceptance and before CEO delivery approval.

Always load:

- `global-core.md`
- `process-core.md`
- `../../process/read-sets/qa-core.md`

This role tests the generated app and records pass/fail evidence. It does not
own product intent, architecture, implementation, or packaging decisions.
