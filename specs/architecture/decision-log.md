version at `../../runs/current/artifacts/architecture/decision-log.md`.

## Decision entry format

Each real entry SHOULD include:

- date
- decision
- alternatives considered

- downstream consequences

- routes
- naming
- packaging
- shared-runtime use
- backend or rules behavior
